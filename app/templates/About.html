Tribute

Tribute is a project that organizes events about Latin American music, which the aim to give a tribute to the different artist that promoted salsa. The main musical period we play in our events is from end of 90's up until now. In special, we focus on salsa dura, which is a rhythm with topics and aesthetics of the urban life in the United States and Latin America.

Goal:

Our goal is to give the audience a well established type of salsa music that is consider a referent into the Latin American Culture as a whole, in order for the dancers to know more about the different salsa styles and themes.
