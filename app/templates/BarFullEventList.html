Las DJ Collective
This female collective of music came with the need of a new form of expression, of the way we feel for life and the way we live for music. Our previous paths, have individually brought us through a wide spectrum of performing arts and music, and within this project << Las Dj Collective>> there is to notice a special bond with Latin American culture and its music (roots&club).

Las Dj Collective is how we, as women, feel about music; of how we reconnect our womanhood and how we reclaim our rights in a globalized, normalized, paralized world.
